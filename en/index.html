About Us

A family devoted to the soil,
passionate about pomegranates.

Founded in 2011, RAZ is a proud producer of high-quality pomegranates and fresh pomegranate products. We are passionate about delivering the finest, most delicious pomegranates straight from our orchards to your door.

Every year we harvest more than 17,000 decares of land, combining our own orchards with carefully selected certified partner farms across the region. By supporting local farmers and sustainable development, we are working our way to industry leadership.

  • Production to international food standards
  • Sustainable farming & a traceable supply chain
  • 6 locations across Türkiye and Germany

From Field to Table

The journey of a pomegranate

Harvested from our contracted orchards, our pomegranates are processed and packaged in a controlled, supervised environment on state-of-the-art production lines that meet international food standards.

  1. 01

    Growing

    In our own orchards, with organic fertiliser and water-saving irrigation.

  2. 02

    Harvesting

    Picked by hand, with care, at peak ripeness.

  3. 03

    Processing

    A hygienic aril-extraction process and cold-press lines.

  4. 04

    Packaging

    Under modified atmosphere, in a range of pack sizes.

  5. 05

    Distribution

    Cold-chain delivery across Türkiye and Europe.
